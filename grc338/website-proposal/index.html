Introduction

Aroma Coffeehouse

The Aroma Kaffeehaus is a cosy place in the centre of Munich, selling the best coffee specialities. A place to switch off and relax.

Target audience

People who want a special coffee experience and appreciate fine sweet specialties are at home with us.

We want to offer the best quality of coffee and sweet specialties to our customers. All customers at our place should feel comfortable and leave the daily stress behind them, even if only for a short time.
